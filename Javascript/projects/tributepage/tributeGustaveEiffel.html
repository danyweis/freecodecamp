Gustave Bönickhausen Eiffel

French civil engineer and architect

Gustave Eiffel was born 15 december 1832 in Dijon and diedon 27 December 1923 (aged 91), while listening to Beethoven's 5th symphony in Paris (France)

The time line of Gustave Eiffel's life and projects:

  • 1832 - born in Dijon, Côte-d'Or, France

School:

  • 1843 - Middle School, Sainte-Barbe
  • 1852 - Central School of Arts and Manufactures, Paris
  • 1855 - Graduation

Buildings and structures:

  • 1862 - Railway station at Toulouse (France)
  • 1864 - Railway station at Agen (France)
  • 1867 - Church of Notre Dame des Champs, Paris (France)
  • 1868 - Synagogue in Rue de Pasarelles , Paris (France)
  • 1886 - Performing Arts Center Lía Bermúdez, Maracaibo, (Venezuela)
  • 1868 - Théâtre les Folies, Paris (France)
  • 1873 - Gasworks, La Paz (Bolivia)
  • 1873 - Gasworks, Tacna (Peru)
  • 1875 - Church of San Marcos, Arica (Chile)
  • 1875 - Cathedrale of San Pedro de Tacna (Peru)
  • 1876 - Lycée Carnot, Paris (France)
  • 1877 - Budapest Nyugati Pályaudvar (Western railway station), Budapest (Hungary)
  • 1877 - Ornamental Fountain of the Three Graces, Moquegua (Peru)
  • 1877 - Ruhnu Lighthouse at Ruhnu island (Estonia)
  • 1881 - San Camilo Market, Arequipa (Peru)
  • 1882 - Farol de São Thomé, Campos (Brazil)
  • 1882 - Mercado Municipal, Manaus (Brazil)
  • 1882 - Grand Hotel Traian, Iasi (Romania)
  • 1886 - Nice Observatory, Nice (France)
  • 1886 - Statue of Liberty, Liberty Island, New York (United States)
  • 1888 - Church in Coquimbo (Chile)
  • 1888 - Colbert Bridge, Dieppe (France)
  • 1889 - Eiffel Tower, Paris (France)
  • 1889 - Paradis Latin theatre, Paris (France)
  • 1890 - Konak Pier, Izmir (Turkey)
  • 1892 - Casa de Fierro, Iquitos (Peru)
  • 1894 - Palacio de Hierro, Orizaba, Veracruz (Mexico)
  • 1897 - Estación Central (railway station), Santiago (Chile)
  • 1897 - Iglesia de Santa Bárbara in Santa Rosalía, Baja California Sur (Mexico)
  • 1906 - La Paz Train Station, La Paz (Bolivia) (today Bus Station)
  • 1911 - Aérodynamique EIFFEL (wind tunnel), Paris (Auteuil) (France)
  • 1912 - The Market, Olhão (Portugal)
  • ...
