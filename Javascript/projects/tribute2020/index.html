Willard Carroll "Will" Smith Jr.

In the late 1980s, Smith achieved modest fame as a rapper under the name The Fresh Prince. In 1990, his popularity increased dramatically when he starred in the NBC television series The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air, which ran for six seasons from 1990 to 1996.


His first major roles after The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air:

Bad Boys is a 1995 American action comedy film directed by Michael Bay.

Independence Day is a 1996 American epic science fiction disaster film directed and co-written by Roland Emmerich.

Men in Black is a 1997 American science fiction action-comedy film directed by Barry Sonnenfeld.

Wild Wild West is a 1999 American steampunk western comedy film co-produced and directed by Barry Sonnenfeld.

I Am Legend is a 2007 American post-apocalyptic action thriller film based on the 1954 novel of the same name by Richard Matheson.

After Earth is a 2013 American post-apocalyptic action film directed by M. Night Shyamalan.
